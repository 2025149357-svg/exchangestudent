Why Study in Indonesia?

Gain experience in a key ASEAN country with growing economic and social influence.

Explore natural landscapes, from beaches and volcanoes to cultural heritage sites.

Engage with local communities and student groups through projects and activities.

Enjoy affordable living costs with rich food culture and daily life experiences.

Important Information

Review these key points before applying for the Indonesia Study Abroad track.

Visa Requirements

Certain programmes may require a student or limited stay visa. Guidance will be provided after acceptance.

Accommodation

Options include student dormitories, shared rentals, or university-arranged housing.

Estimated Costs

Living costs are generally lower than many other destinations, but students should plan a clear budget.

Language

Many courses are delivered in Bahasa Indonesia. Some partners offer English-taught modules and support.

Health & Safety

Health insurance is required. Students must follow local safety guidelines and university policies.

Pre-departure Briefing

All participants attend a briefing on culture, communication, and expectations when living in Indonesia.
