Important Information

Before applying, please review the main requirements and support items for the UK Study Abroad track.

UK Student Visa

A valid UK student visa is required for semester / year abroad and longer programmes. Guidance will be provided after acceptance.

Accommodation

Students may stay in campus halls, private student residences, or approved homestays depending on location and availability.

Financial Planning

You should plan for tuition (if applicable), accommodation, living expenses, transport, and emergency funds. Limited funding may be offered.

Language Requirements

English is the main medium of instruction. Some hosts may request proof of English proficiency (e.g. IELTS / equivalent).

Health & Insurance

Health insurance and compliance with UK healthcare and safety requirements are compulsory for all participants.

Pre-departure Briefing

All students must attend a pre-departure briefing on academic expectations, culture, safety, and living arrangements in the UK.

Application Process

The UK application flow is structured into four stages. You will receive updates at each milestone.

1

Online Application

Submit the online form, choose the UK Study Abroad track, and indicate your preferred host universities and intake.

2

Academic & Eligibility Review

We review your CGPA, programme fit, language requirements, and supporting documents according to host criteria.

3

Nomination & Offer

Shortlisted students are nominated to UK partners and receive formal offer letters or acceptance documents.

4

Visa, Briefing & Departure

After accepting the offer, you proceed with visa application, attend the pre-departure briefing, and finalise your travel plans.
