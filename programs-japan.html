Important Information

Before applying, make sure you understand the key requirements and support provided for all participants.

Student Visa

A valid student visa is required for all academic and internship programs in Japan.

Accommodation

Options include university dormitories, shared apartments, or homestay with local families.

Financial Aid

Selected students may receive scholarships or partial financial assistance from partners.

Language Support

Basic Japanese is helpful but not compulsory. Language support and orientation are provided.

Health & Insurance

Health insurance coverage and safety briefings are mandatory for all participants.

Pre-departure Briefing

You must attend a pre-departure session covering culture, safety, and academic expectations.
